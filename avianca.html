06 — Avianca

Clubes
Avianca

Category
Digital Platform · ATL Campaign
Client
Avianca — via APEX BBDO
Deliverables
Web platform, Billboard, Bus shelter (Mupi), Print ads
Role
Digital Creative · Art Direction · Campaign

Rationale

Travel planning and destination discovery platform for Latin America's leading airline. The challenge was to make Avianca's route network feel personal and aspirational — organizing destinations into curated "clubs" by traveler profile. Alongside the web experience, an integrated ATL campaign extended the identity into out-of-home media, creating cohesive brand presence across digital and physical touchpoints.
