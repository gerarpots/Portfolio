05 — Applaudo

Applaudo
Tech Summit '25

Category

Client
Applaudo
Deliverables
Visual identity, Posters, Digital welcome guide, Environmental design
Event
October 3–5 · El Salvador
Role
Art Direction · Brand System · Design

Rationale

Applaudo's annual client summit needed a visual identity that felt local and premium simultaneously. The concept draws from two forces of the Salvadoran landscape: volcanoes — representing technological magnitude and solid foundation — and waves — representing innovation, movement, and global reach. The result is a fluid, gradient-rich system that feels distinctly Salvadoran while meeting the standards of an international tech event.
